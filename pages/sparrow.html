Sparrow

A sparrow is an omnivorous bird Sparrows generally build their nests in the roof of houses, buildings, bridges and hollows of trees.

Food: seeds, grains, fruits.

Colors: The plumage of the house sparrow is mostly different shades of grey and brown.
The sexes exhibit strong dimorphism: the female is mostly buffish above and below, while the male has boldly coloured head markings, a reddish back, and grey underparts.

life span: ranges between 4–5 years.

weight: 24–39.5 g.
