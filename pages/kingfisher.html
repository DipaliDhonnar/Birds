Kingfisher

Kingfishers are generally brightly colored birds that often fish for their food.Most of these live in warm regions near rivers or lakes.

Food: primarily fish, especially minnows and sticklebacks, but they also eat aquatic insects, freshwater shrimp, and tadpoles.

Weight:Between 9 and 12 g (0.32 and 0.42 oz).

Colors:Blue feathers on their back and reddish or white feathers on their belly.

life span:20 years.
