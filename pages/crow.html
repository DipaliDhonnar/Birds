Crow

Crows are large birds with shiny black feathers.

food:Crows are omnivores, which means they eat nearly anything.
Crows eat small animals such as mammals, amphibians, reptiles, eggs and carrion.
They also eat insects, seeds, grains, nuts, fruit, non-insect arthropods, mollusks.

life span :Crows reach sexual maturity between 3-5 years and can live up to 30 years.
